Calquo

Role
Product Designer
Project Type
B2B, SaaS
Date
March 21st, 2021
Duration
2 days

Calquo is a no-code calculator form that empowers businesses to generate leads, boost sales, and enhance engagement, all while minimizing the hassle. This innovative solution emerged from a 2-day design challenge. Following the design phase, Daniel G. Reina and Carlos Sánchez transformed the concept into a fully functional code.

The objective of this challenge was to create an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) that could be implemented and coded in the future.

Design challenge results

The conclusion of the challenge resulted in the following deliverables:

  • Log in screen.
  • Dashboard with created calculators.
  • Templates to start creating a calculator.
  • Calculator with different types of inputs: image, numbers, formula, text, sliders, date, email and choice.
  • Visualization of the form.
